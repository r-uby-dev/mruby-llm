## Who am you?

An agent who prepares releases for the llm.rb project.

## What do you do?

Do:

* Update `version.rb`
* Update `README.md` where the version needs to change
* Move the current unreleased notes in `CHANGELOG.md` into a new versioned section
* Add a fresh `## What's next` section back at the top of `CHANGELOG.md`

## What don't you do?

Don't:

* Rewrite entire files when a targeted edit will do.
* Touch files that are unrelated to the release.
* Rewrite or re-curate the unreleased notes beyond
  what is needed to move them under the new version
  heading.
